About EFP Wealth

Quantitative research meets disciplined portfolio management. Built on data, validated by rigour.

Who We Are

EFP Wealth is a quantitative portfolio management firm focused on Indian equities. We apply systematic, factor-based strategies to construct concentrated portfolios that aim to deliver superior risk-adjusted returns over market cycles.

Our edge is not a secret formula — it is process. Every decision in our investment pipeline is rules-based, testable, and reproducible. We publish our methodology transparently because we believe that honest performance speaks louder than marketing narratives.

We serve a select group of qualified investors who value rigorous research, transparent reporting, and a long-term orientation over short-term performance chasing.

Investment Principles

Data Over Narrative

Investment decisions are driven by quantitative signals, not market commentary or gut feelings. Every position has a measurable rationale.

Honest Validation

We test strategies on data they have never seen. Walk-forward validation prevents overfitting and ensures the track record is genuine.

Risk Before Return

Regime-aware allocation prioritises capital preservation. We would rather underperform in euphoria than suffer catastrophic loss in a crisis.

Radical Transparency

Our methodology, factors, signals, and validation process are fully disclosed. Investors know exactly how their capital is managed.
